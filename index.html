what is Mobile photography

Mobile photography is the art of photography using a smartphone or mobile device.Defined using the philosophy of 'the best camera is the one that's always with you', mobile photography is the future of the art form. Discreet, intimate and always accessible to capture a moment.Mobile photography is so influential now that camera manufacturers are bringing phones to cameras rather than cameras to phones.
